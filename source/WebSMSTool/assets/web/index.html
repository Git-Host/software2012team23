Usage

To write a short message choose a contact from the contact list on the left side. After clicking on a contact the so called contact-tab will be opened.

In the contact-tab you can write a short message by simple choosing one of the specific contact's numbers and adding some text in the textarea. After clicking on Send your message will be delivered immediately.

At the bottom of the contact-tab you see a list of messages sent or received from the specific contact

After your message has been successfully sent to the contact, the message list of the contact-tab will update itself.
